# Forecasting the Market Capitalization of In-Game Items for CS2

This repository contains a project to analyze and forecast the market capitalization of Counter-Strike 2 skins based on historical data for the period from February 2025 to May 2026.

## 📌 Task Description
The in-game item market for CS2 exhibits high volatility, dependent on Valve updates, esports tournaments (Majors), and external economic factors. The goal of the project is to digitize the historical market capitalization trend, explore the applicability of various mathematical models, and create a realistic forecast for the next 365 days.

## 🛠 Tech Stack Used
* **Python** (analysis and modeling)
* **Pandas & OpenPyXL** (parsing, processing, and structuring data in Excel)
* **NumPy** (mathematical calculations and polynomial regression)
* **Statsmodels & Meta Prophet** (advanced time series forecasting)
* **Matplotlib** (result visualization)

## 📊 Results and Modeling Methods
Three forecasting approaches were used in the study:
1. **Polynomial Regression (Baseline):** Showed the general development trend, but revealed a tendency to overfit at high degrees due to the sharp market rebound in May 2026.
2. **Holt-Winters Model:** Accounted for the long-term trend and short-term monthly fluctuations.
3. **Prophet Algorithm (Meta):** The most adaptive business forecast, taking into account annual market seasonality and constructing confidence intervals (risk corridor for investors).
